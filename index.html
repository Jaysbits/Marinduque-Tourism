Did You Know?

Marinduque is the Geodetic Center of the Philippines

Every official Philippine map is measured from a single point — the Luzon Datum of 1911, located atop Mataas na Bundok hill in Boac, Marinduque. This heart-shaped island doesn't just sit at the center of Filipino hearts; it sits at the geographic center of an entire archipelago.
